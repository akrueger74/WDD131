Hi! My Name is Andrew Krueger and I am a student at BYU-Idaho. I am currently majoring in Cyber Security

This page was created because of my love for the Star Wars universe. I wanted to create a map of some important planets inside the galaxy with brief descriptions. Each planet has been included in both the movies and in some video games as well.

I've always wanted to do an interactive map of the Star Wars galaxy. It reminds me of my childhood game "Star Wars Battlefront 2" that I used to play all the time as a kid with my brother and friends. Creating this map proved more difficult than planned but I learned a lot of new things and also received a lot of help from my tutors and peers.
