Tom Clancy's The Divion: Recruited

February 1, 2022 | Age Range: 16-18 | Genre: Apocalyptic

Set in the world of Tom Clancy’s The Division, Recruited follows a young survivor, Maira Kanhai, as she navigates the chaos of a post-pandemic America where society has collapsed. When she’s chosen to join the Strategic Homeland Division—a last line of defense against lawlessness—she undergoes intense training while grappling with her own doubts and fears. As she embarks on her first mission, Maira is thrust into the heart of a deadly conspiracy that could determine the fate of what remains of the nation, forcing her to choose between survival and doing what’s right.

Star Wars: The Old Republic: Revan

November 15, 2011 | Age Range: 9-12 | Genre: Sci-Fi

Set in the expansive Star Wars: The Old Republic universe, Revan follows the legendary Jedi-turned-Sith-turned-hero as he struggles with lost memories and a dark force looming beyond the known galaxy. Haunted by fragmented visions of his past and an ominous threat he once confronted, Revan embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the secrets buried within his mind. His quest takes him from the Republic’s heart to the storm-ridden world of Dromund Kaas, where he faces a deadly Sith Emperor with the power to alter the fate of the galaxy itself.

Rangers Apprentice: The Ruins of Gorlan

June 26, 1997 | Age Range: 9-12 | Genre: Fantasy

The first book in the Ranger’s Apprentice series introduces Will, a small but quick-witted orphan chosen to train as a Ranger, an elite scout and warrior serving the kingdom of Araluen. Initially skeptical of his new role under the mysterious Ranger Halt, Will soon discovers that his agility and intelligence make him a perfect fit. As the kingdom faces the return of Morgarath, a banished warlord amassing an army, Will must learn the skills of stealth, archery, and survival to help protect his homeland from an impending attack.
